Travis Scott's Early Life

Travis Scott is the stage name of Jacques Webster, a Houston-born hip-hop artist and producer.

Scott grew up in a suburb of Houston and began making music as a teenager. After dropping out of college, Scott moved to Los Angeles and began recording music on his own.
